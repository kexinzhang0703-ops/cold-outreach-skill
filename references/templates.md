# Message Templates

All templates organized by scenario. Customize by replacing bracketed placeholders with specific information.

## LinkedIn Connection Request Templates

### Alumni Connection
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you're also a [School Name] alum working in [Company/Field]. I'm currently exploring opportunities in [Direction] and would love to connect and learn from your journey!
```

**When to use**: Reaching out to someone from the same school/university

**Customization points**:
- School name (be specific: "UCLA" not just "college")
- Their current field/company
- Your target direction/role

---

### Target Company Employee
```
Hi [Name],

I'm very interested in the [Position] role at [Company] and noticed your impressive experience in [Relevant Experience]. I'd love to connect and hear your insights about the team and culture if you're open to it!
```

**When to use**: Reaching out to current employee at company you're targeting

**Customization points**:
- Specific job title you're interested in
- Company name
- Highlight something specific from their profile (project, skill, achievement)

---

### Shared Event/Interest
```
Hi [Name],

I came across your profile after attending [Shared Event] and really appreciated your perspective on [Content/Topic]. I'd love to stay in touch and connect here on LinkedIn!
```

**When to use**: After meeting at conference, webinar, workshop, or shared interest

**Customization points**:
- Specific event name
- Specific topic or insight they shared
- Can reference a conversation you had

---

### General Professional Networking
```
Hi [Name],

I'm currently exploring roles in [Direction/Field] and would love to connect with professionals like you who've walked this path. Looking forward to learning from your journey!
```

**When to use**: Cold outreach with no specific connection point

**Customization points**:
- Your target field/direction
- Can add one sentence about relevant background if needed

---

## Recruiter Outreach Templates

### Job-Specific Outreach (with posting)
```
Subject: Application for [Position] - [Your Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out regarding the [Position] role at [Company] (link: [job posting URL]).

I believe I'm a strong fit because:
• [Qualification 1 matching job requirement]
• [Qualification 2 matching job requirement]
• [Qualification 3 matching job requirement]

I'm particularly excited about [Company]'s [specific initiative/mission/achievement] and would love to discuss how my experience in [relevant area] could contribute to the team.

I've attached my resume and cover letter. Would you be available for a brief conversation about this opportunity?

Thank you,
[Your Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
[LinkedIn URL]
```

**When to use**: Applying to specific job posting, have recruiter's email

**Key elements**:
- Always include job posting link
- Match 2-3 qualifications to actual job description requirements
- Attach resume + cover letter
- Show genuine interest in company (research required)

---

### General Recruiter Inquiry
```
Subject: [Your Role] Exploring Opportunities in [Field]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], a [your title/background] with strong experience in [key skills]. I'm exploring new opportunities in [target field/roles] and would love to connect.

My background includes:
• [Key achievement/experience 1]
• [Key achievement/experience 2]

I'm excellent at interviewing and very motivated to find the right role. Do you have any positions that match my experience?

My resume is attached. Happy to discuss further.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: Cold outreach to recruiters without specific job in mind

**Keep it concise**: Recruiters are busy, get to the point quickly

**Highlight interview skills**: Recruiters earn commission when you get hired

---

### Short Recruiter LinkedIn Message
```
Hello [Name],

I'm [Your Name], a [role/expertise] with strong skills in [key area]. I'm excellent at interviewing and actively exploring opportunities in [field].

Do you have any openings that match my experience? Here's my resume: [attachment]

Can we talk?

Thank you,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: LinkedIn InMail or when character count matters

**Ultra-concise**: Recruiters handle high volume, respect their time

---

## Coffee Chat Templates

### Initial Request
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you're working at [Company] as [Their Role], and I'm currently exploring opportunities in [Your Target Field].

I'd love to learn from your experience, particularly about [specific aspect - e.g., "transitioning into this field" or "the culture at [Company]"].

Would you have 20 minutes next week for a quick coffee chat? I'm happy to work around your schedule.

Thanks so much,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: First message requesting informational interview

**Key points**:
- Specific time request (20 minutes)
- Specific topic you want to discuss
- Flexibility on timing

---

### Scheduling Confirmation
```
Hi [Name],

Thank you for agreeing to chat! I've sent you a calendar invite for [Day, Time] with the Zoom link.

Here's the link again: [Zoom URL]

Looking forward to speaking with you!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: After they agree to meet, before the actual chat (2 days before)

**Critical**: Send calendar invite AND confirm via message

---

### Day-of Reminder
```
Hi [Name],

Looking forward to our chat at [Time] today!

Here's the Zoom link: [URL]

See you soon!

[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: Morning of the chat day (8-10 AM)

**Purpose**: Reduces no-shows, shows professionalism

---

### Thank You Follow-up
```
Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to speak with me yesterday. I really appreciated your insights on [specific topic they discussed], particularly [specific detail/advice].

I'm going to [action you're taking based on their advice]. I'll keep you updated on my progress.

Thanks again for your generosity!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: Within 24 hours after coffee chat

**Key elements**:
- Reference specific insight they shared (shows you listened and took notes)
- Mention action you'll take based on their advice
- Keep door open for future contact

---

### Relationship Maintenance (2-4 weeks later)
```
Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well! I wanted to share [relevant article/news about their industry/company] and thought you might find it interesting.

Also wanted to update you: [brief progress update on your job search or advice they gave].

Thanks again for your time and guidance!

[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: 2-4 weeks after initial chat, or when you see relevant content

**Purpose**: Stay on their radar without asking for anything

---

## Follow-up Templates

### After Networking Event
```
Hi [Name],

It was great meeting you at [Event Name] yesterday! I really enjoyed our conversation about [specific topic you discussed].

As I mentioned, I'm exploring opportunities in [your field]. I'd love to stay in touch and continue the conversation.

[Optional: Would you be open to a 20-minute coffee chat in the next few weeks?]

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: Within 48 hours of meeting someone at event

**Key**: Reference specific conversation point (they meet many people)

---

### General Stay-in-Touch
```
Hi [Name],

I hope you're doing well! I came across [article/news/insight] and immediately thought of our conversation about [topic].

[Brief personal update if relevant: "I wanted to share that I recently [achievement/progress]"]

Hope all is well with you!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**When to use**: Every 2-3 months with valuable contacts

**Purpose**: Maintain relationship without asking for favors

---

## Customization Guidelines

### Strong Openings
✅ "I noticed you're working on [specific project/initiative]"
✅ "Your post about [topic] really resonated with me"
✅ "I saw we share [specific connection point]"

❌ "I hope this message finds you well"
❌ "Sorry to bother you"
❌ "I know you're busy but..."

### Clear Asks
✅ "Would you have 20 minutes next week?"
✅ "Are you open to a brief chat?"
✅ "Could we schedule a quick call?"

❌ "Would love to pick your brain"
❌ "Can I ask you some questions?"
❌ "Let me know if you'd be willing to help"

### Professional Closings
✅ "Thanks so much"
✅ "Looking forward to connecting"
✅ "I appreciate your time"

❌ "Thanks in advance"
❌ "Hope to hear from you"
❌ Overly formal: "I remain at your disposal"

---

## Template Selection Decision Tree

```
START: What's your goal?

├─ Connect with someone on LinkedIn
│  ├─ They're alumni → Use Alumni template
│  ├─ They work at target company → Use Target Company template
│  ├─ You met at event → Use Shared Event template
│  └─ No connection point → Use General Professional template
│
├─ Contact a recruiter
│  ├─ Have specific job posting → Use Job-Specific template (email preferred)
│  └─ General exploration → Use General Inquiry template
│
├─ Request informational interview
│  ├─ First request → Use Initial Request template
│  ├─ They agreed, scheduling → Use Scheduling Confirmation
│  ├─ Day of chat → Use Day-of Reminder
│  └─ After the chat → Use Thank You Follow-up
│
└─ Follow up
   ├─ After networking event → Use After Event template
   └─ Maintain relationship → Use Stay-in-Touch template
```
