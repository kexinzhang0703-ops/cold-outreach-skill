# Usage Examples

This document provides real-world examples of using the Cold Outreach Skill.

## Example 1: Alumni Networking (LinkedIn Message)

### Input

**User uploads resume**:
- Background: Software Engineer at Startup XYZ
- Education: Stanford University, BS Computer Science
- Skills: Python, React, Machine Learning

**Recipient information**:
- Name: Jennifer Wu
- Position: Senior Product Manager at Meta
- Company: Meta
- Experience: 5 years in product management, previously at Google
- Relationship: Stanford alumni

**Format choice**: LinkedIn Message (300 characters)

### Generated Output

```
Hi Jennifer, I'm also a Stanford alum currently working as a software engineer. 
I saw your PM journey from Google to Meta and would love to learn from your 
experience. Would you have 20 minutes for a quick chat?
```

**Character count**: 217/300 ✓

**User uploads resume**:
- Background: Marketing Manager at Tech Company
- Education: UC Berkeley, BA Economics
- Skills: Growth Marketing, Data Analytics, A/B Testing

**Recipient information**:
- Name: David Chen
- Position: Head of Growth at Stripe
- Company: Stripe
- Experience: Built growth team from 0 to 50 people, scaled fintech products
- Relationship: No existing relationship

**Format choice**: Email

### Generated Output

```
Subject: Growth Marketing Professional Interested in Stripe

Hi David,

I came across your profile and was really impressed by your work building 
Stripe's growth team from the ground up. I'm particularly interested in how 
you've scaled growth strategies in the fintech space.

I'm currently a Marketing Manager with 4 years of experience in growth 
marketing, specializing in data-driven experimentation and user acquisition. 
My recent work includes scaling a B2B SaaS product from 10K to 100K users 
through strategic A/B testing and channel optimization.

I'd love to learn from your experience and hear about the growth challenges 
you're tackling at Stripe. Would you be open to a 20-minute conversation in 
the next few weeks?

Happy to work around your schedule!

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Common Patterns

### For Alumni Connections
- **Opening**: "I'm also a [School] alum..."
- **Context**: Current work + exploration direction
- **Ask**: 20-minute chat
- **Length**: Usually fills 180-220 characters

### For No-Relationship Outreach
- **Opening**: "I came across your profile and was impressed by..."
- **Interest**: Specific aspect of their work/company
- **Connection**: Your relevant background
- **Ask**: Brief conversation
- **Length**: Email ~150 words, LinkedIn ~250 characters

### For Recruiter Messages
- **Subject**: Clear job title or general exploration
- **Structure**: Bullet points for qualifications
- **Always include**: Resume attachment, contact info
- **Length**: 100-150 words

### For Follow-Ups
- **Timing**: Within 24 hours of meeting
- **Include**: Specific detail they mentioned
- **Action**: What you'll do based on their advice
- **Keep**: Under 100 words

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific with recipient information**: The more context you provide, the better the personalization
2. **Update your resume regularly**: Keep it current for best matching
3. **Choose the right format**: LinkedIn for quick connects, Email for deeper outreach
4. **Customize the output**: Feel free to adjust the generated message
5. **Follow the workflow**: Don't skip the information collection step
